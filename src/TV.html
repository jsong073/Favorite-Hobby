TV Shows

I spend a lot of my time on Netflix. Here are some of my favorite shows.

  • Parks and Recreation



    Probably my favorite sitcom. A gang of government agents trying to improve the parks in their town. I like it because it's funny, but everyone in the show is still mostly nice to each other.

  • Avatar: The Last Airbender


    One of my favorite shows of all time. A boy hidden in an iceberg for over 100 years must learn to control all four elements and defeat a tyrannical king. Great for both adults and kids.
